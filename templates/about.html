About the Project

PneumoAI is a final year engineering project focused on applying Deep Learning to medical diagnostics.

Project Goal

The primary objective is to provide an accessible tool for early detection of pneumonia using chest X-ray images, assisting healthcare professionals with a reliable second opinion.

The Model

We use the ResNet50 convolutional neural network, pre-trained on ImageNet and fine-tuned on thousands of chest X-ray images. This architecture is renowned for its ability to handle vanishing gradients in deep networks.

Interpretability with Grad-CAM

To ensure the system is trustworthy, we integrated Grad-CAM. This technique produces heatmaps that highlight the specific areas in the X-ray image that influenced the model's decision, allowing doctors to verify the findings.
